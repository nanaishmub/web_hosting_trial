Vampire Say What

Bud Jablonski (Jamie Foxx) works as a freelance vampire-hunter in the San Fernando Valley. When his estranged wife (Meagan Good) demands money for their daughter’s braces, he is forced to team up with union rep Seth (Dave Franco) to earn the big bucks.

Video Game Mania

Movie Name: Free Guy

Release Date: 13th Aug, 2021

About Free Guy

When a bank teller discovers he's actually a background player in an open-world video game, he decides to become the hero of his own story -- one that he can rewrite himself. In a world where there's no limits, he's determined to save the day his way before it's too late, and maybe find a little romance with the coder who conceived him.

My Experience in the Video-game World

This movie is forever carved in my memories. When you first see this movie you're like, "Oh, it's a video game movie." But once you've watched it from beginning to end you'll be more like, "OHH!!! IT'S A VIDEO GAME MOVIE!!!" Guy is such a captivating character who shows that anything is possible. His adventures in Free City are by far one of my favourite.
